About Me

What do I do?

I am a Full Stack Software Engineer with focus on JavaScript & Python and Frameworks such as Vue, React and Django. Having started as a Frontend Developer; I have a very artistic approach and pay close attention to even the smallest details.

How do I do it?

I always put myself in the shoes of the Client/Non-Programmer. Obviously, if I have a Mockup and Idea that needs to be implemented, I will follow that. I just imagine, can a non-programmer use this? Can a Pensioner who has no idea about Technology know what is what? Therefore, I try to make the User Interface & Experience as easy and pretty as possible.

Why hire me?

Having worked in many different types of Companies and experienced different work cultures from around the world; I bring a very large skill-set that can be useful to my new potential team. Also, I have a very open and extroverted personality that allows me to adapt to a new team & environment very quickly. Plus, I work well alone as well as with a team I.e. I am very flexible.
